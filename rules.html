League Rules

Match Rules

1. PENALTY AREA

All outfield players are allowed inside the penalty area.

2. HEAD HEIGHT RULE

The ball is allowed to go above head height at any time.

3. BACK PASSES

Players may pass back to their goalkeeper.

4. KICK OFF

The referee starts the match by throwing the ball into the air at the centre circle.

5. NO HALF TIME

Matches are played straight through unless conditions require halves.

6. RESTART AFTER GOAL

The goalkeeper restarts play with a throw or kick out.

7. THROW INS

Throw-ins are replaced with kick-ins.

8. GOAL KICKS

Goal kicks are replaced with throw-outs or kick-outs.

9. CORNERS

Standard corner kicks apply.

10. SLIDE TACKLES

Slide tackles are not permitted.

Inside box = Penalty

Outside box = Indirect free kick

11. FREE KICKS & SET PIECES

Inside box = Penalty

Outside box = Indirect free kick

Ball must be stationary.

12. PENALTIES

Taken from edge of the box.

One step allowed.

13. PENALTIES AWARDED FOR

• More than 6 players on pitch

• Fouls inside the box

• Handball in box

14. GOALKEEPER RULES

• Cannot leave penalty area

• Can throw or kick the ball

15. SUBSTITUTES

Rolling substitutions allowed.

16. TIME WASTING

Results in indirect free kicks or sin bins.

17. SIN BIN (Blue Card)

2-minute removal from play.

18. RED CARD

Serious fouls may result in suspension.

19. REFEREE

The referee’s decision is final.

20. SERIOUS MISCONDUCT

Reported to County FA.

League Information

LEAGUE POINTS

2 points for a win and 1 point for a draw.

MATCH DURATION

30-minute matches.

COMPETITIVE MATCHMAKING

Dominant teams may be removed to maintain fairness and balance.

SQUAD RULES

Teams may use unlimited players across the season.

Maximum 10 players per match night.

Players may only play for one team unless approved as a guest player.

CLOTHING

No jeans or unsuitable footwear.

Goalkeepers may wear caps.

Bibs required unless teams have matching kits.

Boots must suit 3G surfaces i.e. no metal studs.
