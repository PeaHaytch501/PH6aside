League Rules

Match Rules

1. PENALTY AREA

All outfield players are ALLOWED inside the penalty area.

2. HEAD HEIGHT RULE

The ball is ALLOWED to go above head height at any time in the match.

3. BACK PASSES

All outfield players are ALLOWED to pass the ball back to their goalkeeper at any time in the match.

4. KICK OFF

The Referee will start the match by throwing the ball in the air at the centre of the pitch.

5. NO HALF TIME

All matches are straight through unless conditions require two halves.

6. KICK OFF AFTER A GOAL

The goalkeeper restarts play with a THROW OUT or KICK OUT.

7. THROW INS

Throw-ins are replaced with a KICK IN.

8. GOAL KICKS

Goal kicks are replaced with a THROW OUT or KICK OUT.

9. CORNERS

Standard corner kicks apply.

10. SLIDE TACKLES

Slide tackles are NOT permitted.

Inside box = Penalty | Outside box = Indirect free kick

11. FREE KICKS & SET PIECES

Inside box = Penalty

Outside box = Indirect free kick

12. PENALTIES

Taken from edge of the box

13. PENALTIES WILL BE AWARDED

• More than 6 players

• Fouls in the box

14. GOALKEEPER

Cannot leave penalty area

15. SUBSTITUTES

Rolling substitutions allowed

16. TIME WASTING

Results in free kicks or sin bins

17. SIN BIN (Blue Card)

2-minute removal

18. DISMISSAL (Red Card)

Serious fouls = suspension

19. REFEREE

Decision is final

20. SERIOUS MISCONDUCT

Reported to County FA

League Information

LEAGUE POINTS

The scoring system is 2 points for a win and 1 point for a draw.

MATCH DURATION

30 minute matches.

COMPETITIVE MATCHMAKING

To keep the league competitive, overly dominant teams will be removed to preserve fair play, balance, and engagement for all players.

SQUAD

We operate a flexible squad system. Teams may use as many players as they wish during the season, however only 10 players may be used for any one match night.

Players may only play for ONE team in the league unless they become a guest player just for the night.

If your team is short you may use guest players, but this must be cleared by the referee and opposition before kick-off.

CLOTHING

Outfield players must not play with jeans, shoes or without a shirt.

The goalkeeper is ALLOWED to play with a cap.

All teams must use provided bibs unless wearing matching kits.

Shinpads are advised but not mandatory.

Boots must be suitable for 3G surfaces.

Inside box = Penalty

Outside box = Indirect free kick

12. PENALTIES

Taken from edge of the box

One step allowed

Goalkeeper must stay on line

13. PENALTIES WILL BE AWARDED

• More than 6 players on pitch

• Fouls inside the box

• Handball in box

• Goalkeeper leaving area

14. GOALKEEPER

No volleys from hands

Cannot leave penalty area

Can throw or kick the ball

15. SUBSTITUTES

Rolling substitutions allowed

Player must leave pitch before replacement enters

16. TIME WASTING

Time wasting results in indirect free kicks or sin bins

17. SIN BIN (Blue Card)

2-minute removal

Second blue card = suspension

18. DISMISSAL (Red Card)

Serious fouls = suspension

Violence/abuse = longer bans

19. REFEREE

The referee’s decision is final
